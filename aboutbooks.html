About Us


Welcome to Books by Codetek Books, your destination for affordable and diverse reading materials. We specialize in providing high-quality used books sold by weight, making literature accessible to everyone.

Our mission is to promote reading culture by offering a wide range of genres and authors at competitive prices. Whether you are an avid reader or just starting your reading journey, we have something for you.


At Books by Codetek Books, we believe in the power of storytelling and its ability to enrich lives. Each book in our selection is carefully chosen to ensure both quality and variety, encouraging exploration and discovery among our readers. We aim to foster a community of book lovers by creating an inclusive space where everyone can find literary treasures to enjoy and cherish.

With a commitment to sustainability, we promote the reuse of books as a way to reduce environmental impact. By selling books by weight, we not only offer affordable options but also contribute to the preservation of resources.

Join us in celebrating the joy of reading at Books by Codetek Books, where every page holds a new adventure waiting to be discovered.
